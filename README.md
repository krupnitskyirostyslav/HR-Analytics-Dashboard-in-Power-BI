# HR Analytics

Tools: Power BI Desktop, Power Query, DAX, Data Visualization Best Practices

## Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive HR Analytics Dashboard designed to monitor workforce health, employee engagement, satisfaction, work-life balance, performance, and employee attrition.

The dashboard was developed to answer critical HR business questions and provide actionable insights for workforce planning and employee retention strategies.

## Business Questions

The dashboard focuses on the following analytical questions:

### 1. What is the overall level of employee engagement and satisfaction?

Measure company-wide employee sentiment using:

- Average Engagement Score
- Average Satisfaction Score
- Total Employee Count

### 2. Are there departments with below-average engagement or satisfaction?

Identify departments that fall below organizational benchmarks.

Metrics include:

- Engagement by Department
- Satisfaction by Department
- Company Average Reference Lines

This helps HR teams prioritize improvement initiatives.


### 3. How has work-life balance changed over the last year?

Track monthly trends in employee work-life balance scores.

Insights include:

- Positive or negative trends
- Seasonal fluctuations
- Periods requiring management attention

### 4. How many employees have been lost?

Analyze workforce attrition through employee status categories:

- Active
- Future Start
- Leave of Absence
- Voluntarily Terminated
- Terminated for Cause


### 5. What were the performance ratings of employees who left?

Evaluate the relationship between employee performance and turnover.

Performance categories:

- Fully Meets
- Exceeds
- Needs Improvement
- PIP (Performance Improvement Plan)
